At PACE Physical Therapy, we want to maximize your time with us. Therefore, we offer our paperwork online, so you can complete it in the privacy of your own home. Prior to your first visit, please download the Patient Intake Forms and bring them with you to your first visit, along with your insurance information and photo ID.

Before You Visit

  • Have in writing key past medical history, previous treatments, current medications, and other important information.
  • Please call our office to verify your insurance benefits.
  • Bring with you a copy of your insurance card and a photo ID.
  • Make a list of questions that you have to discuss on your first visit.
  • List signs and symptoms that can be of importance to the therapist.
  • Arrive 15 minutes early for your first appointment.

What to Expect on Your First Visit

  • You will meet our front desk staff upon your arrival. They will help you with your paperwork and update you with your insurance information, benefits, and eligibility and get authorization if needed, depending on your insurance carrier.
  • You will be assigned to an experienced licensed physical therapist to work with you.
  • Your physical therapist will evaluate your condition and create a customized plan of physio care for you, based on the latest evidence of physical therapy clinical research.
  • You should be expecting a follow-up call within 24 to 48 hours after your initial visit to get your feedback.
